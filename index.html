AI for Healthcare · Medical LLMs · EHR Modeling

Reliable AI systems for healthcare research.

I am a master's student at Peking University, advised by Prof. Liantao Ma. My research focuses on AI for Healthcare, especially medical large language models, electronic health record modeling, multi-agent collaboration, and reusable benchmark/toolkit infrastructure for clinical data analysis.

Medical Large Language Models

Building and evaluating LLM-driven systems for clinical prediction, reasoning, retrieval, and decision support.

Electronic Health Record Modeling

Modeling longitudinal, sparse, and multimodal EHR data with attention to clinical reliability and reuse.

Benchmarks, Toolkits, and Protocols

Creating open resources that make healthcare AI evaluation more reproducible and practically useful.
