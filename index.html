构建面向真实医疗场景的 AI 系统 Building reliable AI systems for real healthcare settings

我目前是北京大学软件与微电子学院硕士研究生，导师为 马连韬老师。我的研究聚焦于 AI for Healthcare，重点关注医疗大语言模型、电子病历建模，以及面向医疗研究社区的评测基准与工具协议。我希望让临床数据建模与医疗 AI 系统更可靠、可复用、可落地。 I am a master's student at Peking University, advised by Prof. Liantao Ma. My research focuses on AI for Healthcare, with an emphasis on medical large language models, electronic health record modeling, and benchmark or protocol building for the healthcare AI community. I aim to make clinical AI systems more reliable, reusable, and practically deployable.

当前关注 Current Themes

围绕多智能体协作、检索增强、临床预测与医疗工作流自动化，探索 LLM 与传统建模方法在真实医疗任务中的结合方式。 Current work centers on multi-agent collaboration, retrieval augmentation, clinical prediction, and workflow automation for medical tasks by combining LLMs with conventional modeling methods.

研究主题 Research Themes

围绕医疗 AI 的方法、系统与基础设施展开。 Methodological and systems-oriented work around healthcare AI.

医疗大语言模型 Medical Large Language Models

关注多智能体协作、知识检索和医疗任务推理，探索 LLM 在临床数据分析与决策支持中的作用。 Studying multi-agent collaboration, knowledge retrieval, and medical reasoning to improve LLM-based support for clinical analysis and decision making.

电子病历建模 Electronic Health Record Modeling

面向时序 EHR 数据开展预测建模，关注稀疏性、缺失值、多模态融合和临床可解释性等问题。 Building predictive models on longitudinal EHR data with attention to sparsity, missing values, multimodal fusion, and clinical interpretability.

评测基准与协议 Benchmarks, Toolkits, and Protocols

构建可复用的数据处理协议、基准任务和开放工具，支持医疗 AI 方法的系统比较与社区协作。 Creating reusable protocols, benchmark tasks, and open tools to enable systematic comparison and community collaboration in healthcare AI.

系统落地视角 Practical Systems Perspective

强调方法在真实医疗任务中的可靠性与可迁移性，兼顾研究创新与实际工作流的适配。 Focusing on reliable and transferable methods that fit real medical workflows while retaining research novelty.
