[About Me] I am currently a master student at PKU School of Software & Microelectronics, advised by Prof. Liantao Ma. My research primarily focuses on AI for Healthcare, with emphasis on the following areas: (1) Medical Large Language Models (LLMs): Developing LLM agents and multimodal LLMs for medical applications; and (2) Healthcare Modeling: Clinical predictive modeling using electronic health record (EHR) data.
